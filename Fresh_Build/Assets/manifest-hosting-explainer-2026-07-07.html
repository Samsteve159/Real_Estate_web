Your question: can we cut the domain cost?

Yes — you're not locked in. We can keep your domain, move it to a cheaper provider (same name, lower yearly fee), or use the free first-year domain included with Deluxe. Changing it later is easy — no rebuild needed.

Your question: the monthly email charge

Right now email is a separate paid add-on (GoDaddy charges from $3.50 per mailbox each month). Web Hosting Deluxe includes email free — so we cancel that add-on and your email keeps working at no extra cost.
