**Describe use case or workflow of integration (how will this improve experience for the customer)***
> Manifest Real Estate is replacing its existing website with a new one that runs live off their VaultRE
> account. Listings entered in Vault will appear on the website automatically, with no double data-entry, so
> the site is always accurate and current. Website enquiries and appraisal requests are written back into
> Vault as contacts/leads, so the sales team receives them directly in their CRM and can respond quickly.
> Net benefit: buyers and sellers see up-to-date listings and get faster follow-up, and the agency saves
> admin time by capturing every lead in one place.

**Describe how you will use the API***
> Server-side integration only. The website's backend (Node) calls the API using our X-Api-Key plus the
> client's Bearer access token; the key is never exposed in the browser. It periodically reads the client's
> active listings (details, status and media) and caches them locally for display, and creates contact
> records in Vault when a visitor submits a website enquiry or appraisal request. Read-mostly and low
> frequency.

**What endpoints are required?***
> Read (GET): the account's property listings across the types the agency handles (residential, commercial and
> rural/lifestyle), both for sale and for lease, including listing details, status and media/images, to
> display on the website. Write (POST): create contact / enquiry records for website leads. Also GET /scopes
> to confirm the access token's granted scopes. All per the VaultRE v1.3 documentation
> (docs.api.vaultre.com.au) — happy to confirm the exact paths with your tech team during review.

**What are the usage requirements? (e.g. estimated volume)***
> Low volume, read-mostly. Following your guidance, we will not call the API live on each page load —
> listings are synced periodically (roughly every 15–30 minutes) and cached locally, so normal operation is
> well within the 10 requests/second and 10,000 requests/day limits (on the order of a few hundred requests
> per day). Contact writes occur only on website enquiries (a handful per day). We handle HTTP 429 with
> back-off.

**Would you like to discuss a deep integration or strategic opportunity with us?***
> **No** (recommended — this is a standard single-account website listings + leads integration, not a
> platform partnership). Only pick "Yes" if Akshay wants a sales/partnership conversation with MRI.

## Confirmed technical facts (from Vault's guide, for reference)
- **Auth:** every request needs `X-Api-Key: {our key}` **and** `Authorization: Bearer {client access token}`. HTTPS only.
- **API Key** is issued to us (the integrator) on approval; the **Access Token** is generated by the client (Akshay), one per customer, with scopes he chooses (listings read + contacts write). Check granted scopes via `GET /scopes`.
- **Rate limit:** 10 req/sec, 10,000 req/day (resets 00:00 UTC); 429 when exceeded → cache + sync periodically (their explicit recommendation).
