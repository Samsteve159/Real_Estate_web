The Situation

A solid agent, held back by a template

Manifest Real Estate serves Melbourne's inner- and outer-west (Altona North, South Kingsville, Newport, Sunbury). The current site does the basics — but it's a locked WordPress template with zero differentiation, and the agent can't even edit it yet.

Today's website

  • Generic template look — indistinguishable from every other agency
  • No AI features; nothing that captures or qualifies leads automatically
  • Listings & theme controlled by a provider — owner can't make changes
  • Visitors browse, then leave. No reason to hand over their details.

The companion build

  • A striking, custom-designed experience that stands out instantly
  • Two AI tools that actively generate & qualify seller and buyer leads
  • Built separately on our own domain — no need for site rights today
  • Designed to drop into the real site later, or become the new site

The Product

Two tools that earn their keep

🏡
Seller lead magnet

Instant Home Valuation

A visitor enters their address and gets an instant, indicative price range — generated by AI reasoning over Manifest's own recently-sold comparables. The estimate is the hook; capturing a hot seller's contact details is the prize. Every valuation becomes a qualified appraisal opportunity in the agent's inbox.

💬
Buyer engagement · 24/7

AI Buyer Concierge

A chat assistant that knows every current listing and suburb, answers buyer questions any hour, searches properties in plain English ("3-bed under $900k near a train station"), and books inspections — capturing each enquiry as a lead automatically.
